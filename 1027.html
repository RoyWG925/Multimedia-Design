Date: October 27, 2024

Today’s Experience:  
Today, I discussed the details of a group project with a friend, but I realized I was a bit assertive and didn’t give them much chance to share their opinions.

Reflection and Thoughts:
Looking back, I think I was too focused on expressing my own ideas, which may have made my friend feel somewhat withdrawn. I noticed that, at times, my desire to quickly solve issues can unintentionally lead me to overlook others’ perspectives or feelings.  

Improvement Plan:  
Next time we have a discussion, I’ll intentionally allow more time for them to share their thoughts and listen patiently. I believe this will create a more balanced conversation and foster a better cooperative atmosphere.
